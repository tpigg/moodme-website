ABOUT PRODUCT

MoodMe is the healthiest way to change your mood.

We've combined VR immersive abilities and proven mindfulness technique to help you to grow your emotional intelligence, live more happy and emotionally balanced life. We are providing clients with film-like 20 min experiences which can help them go from current mood to any desired one.

How it works: you open the app, choose the mood you want to be in and enjoy your teleportation to greatest natural places when you meet your teacher who guide you through your process of mood change. Our teachers are using breathing, basic stretching and meditation technics from Yoga and Tai Chi to help you to live more happy and joyful life.

When you can use it:

  • you are stressed in the middle of the day and want to recharge yourself and be happy again
  • you are tired in the evening and want to be relaxed
  • or be energize in the morning
  • or be more in creative flow
  • or even overcome your procrastination
